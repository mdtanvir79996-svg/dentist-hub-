Dr Rena Uberoi

Founder and Principal Dental Dr Rena Uberoi received her dental qualifications from the prestigious Guy’s Dental Hospital, London, in 1993. Over an illustrative career of over three decades in general dentistry, she has been aiding patients with their dental problems and concerns.


Dr Uberoi has a special interest in the field of functional aesthetics and occlusion. She is one of very few dentists trained in the use of a digital bite analysis machine called a Tekscan.
